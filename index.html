Features

Everything your suite needs.
Nothing it doesn't.

Built from the ground up for suite and modern academic institutions worldwide.

Showcase your academic research

Easily upload, categorize, and share your journals, conference papers, and reports with a global audience directly from your portal.

  • ✔ Upload PDF, DOCX, and presentation files
  • ✔ Categorize by Journal, Conference, or Book
  • ✔ Tag works with DOI and custom keywords
  • ✔ Seamless ORCID profile integration

Run your classes efficiently

Create public or private courses, manage student enrollments, and handle assignment submissions in one centralized dashboard.

  • ✔ Create courses with syllabus and materials
  • ✔ Privacy controls (Public vs. Approved Students)
  • ✔ Handle assignment uploads and grading
  • ✔ Dedicated portal for enrolled students

A central hub for study materials

Provide your students and public visitors with direct access to lecture notes, presentation slides, and critical datasets.

  • ✔ Upload and organize teaching aids
  • ✔ Categorize by Lecture Notes, Datasets, etc.
  • ✔ Easy download access for visitors
  • ✔ Integrated resource request forms

Build your academic voice

Share academic commentary, industry insights, and updates with an integrated, easy-to-use blogging engine—no coding required.

  • ✔ Rich-text WYSIWYG article editor
  • ✔ Organize posts with tags and categories
  • ✔ Moderated comment sections
  • ✔ Featured posts on your home page
